About Us

The Cooperative Gaming Clan is all about fostering teamwork and fun through cooperative board and video games. Join us to explore a world of collaborative gaming!

Gaming meetups

The Cooperative Gaming Clan meets in person once a month to play boardgames. We meet in the Shift Click Community Hub on the 3rd Wednesday of the month from 7pm to 11pm.

Pizza and refrshments are provided, altough you are also welcome to bring your own!
